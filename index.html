Introduction:

Community Coin is a Cardano-based project that aims to create an NFT series that dynamically displays your community interactions on Twitter! #CardanoCommunity #ADAChain

This generator uses data that is publicly available, you don't need to log in or provide your password . It determines who you interact with the most and it's all based off of your most recent tweets and likes. Note that this is based off of YOUR interactions, not when you get liked, RT'ed, QT'ed, or @'ed. Hopefully in the future I can develop a version of this to work that way, but it would likely require more data from you. Additionally, private accounts will not display - that's kinda how privacy works, lol. Then, it counts the number of interactions you've had with each account and then assigns them a score for the coin generator based off the table below. The accounts are sorted into the coin with higher scores closer to the inner circle
