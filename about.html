Background

First, thanks for checking out my page! I think it's so cool that this is out on the worldwide web and I not only built it, but have the power to change it! crazy! I've always wanted to learn how to do this, but never got around to it. Enter blockchain. I've been inspired by the technology and want to learn more, eventually being able to code in Haskell - it'll be a long journey but an exciting one. I think blockchain will make coding significantly mainstream, eventually being taught in schools at young ages like languages are today. Gotta keep up with kids these days! haha!

I work in healthcare IT currently, so nto totally foreign to coding but it wasn't somethign I did everyday. I have enrolled in a course to learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript and this webpage is the result of that course. My hope is that one day I can work as a Cardano devloper, but thats far in the future.

I came up with the idea for this NFT project prior to enrolling in the course and had no coding knowledge. I was checking out many of the cNFT projects that were emerging on the Cardano blockchain and some really caught my attention - the were dynamic, not just an animation runnig on a loop but living on-chain. Then I stumbled upon something totally outside of the crypto-twittersphere. I saw this cool graphic that displayed the profile avatars of other user's around your avatar in the center, and it was customizable! Then the idea just popped into my head. Wouldn't it be cool to have the avatars be the pips on the Cardano logo on a coin that could be a dynamic NFT that lived entirely on-chain. It would be a great community connector!
