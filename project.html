Community

Through connecting & supporting each other, the Cardano Community grows stronger. This NFT series is not a silly 10k cartoon animal thats been done 100 times over, sometimes on multiple blockchains. It's not a random drop from some rarity table that the CNFT flippers crave - its all about you and your connection to the community. Whether its through sharing protocol updates, development resources, pool statuses, general knowledge, or even just hilarious GIFs with each other, we're forming a fantastic web of decentralized information within a dynamic and growing group of individuals. We all have our own thoughts and opinions and this series aims to be a beneficial representation of that.

Stability

This is not a get rich quick scheme - the goal is to bolster the community and have fun doing so. There are many stake pool operators that run Cardano, and they need our support to run the network. The idea is that this series builds up enough of an 'anchor' for pools to stay operational and mint blocks. In doing so, hopefully more delegation is attracted and the network becomes stronger and better for it.


Cost Breakdown

Initial Cost 10 ADA
"Anchor Stake" (50%) 5 ADA staked to Community Pools
Catalyst Treasury (20%) 2 ADA to be granted to developers through Catalyst vote
Min UTXO (20% returned to YOU) 2 ADA back to you along with your NFT (protocol requirement)
Fees and Maintenance (10%) 1 ADA to pay for tx fees and dust kept by team
Total Cost 8 ADA

Your Voice & Your Choice

I find it really annoying when you mint an NFT and don't get one you like - thats the case with most of projects out there. People cope with it by creating subgroups within the collectors, like the gold-skins or the smokers. Rather than forcing yourself to be happy with the result, with Community Coin you get to design yours! You can pick the 'traits' or whatever you want to call them - Background color or pattern, outline, and other extras are all up to you.
